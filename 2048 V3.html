How to Play

⬆⬇⬅➡
Move the tiles
Use your arrow keys (or swipe on mobile) to slide all tiles in that direction at once.
✕
Tiles merge
When two tiles with the same number collide, they merge into one tile with their combined value.
🏆
Reach 2048… and beyond
The classic goal is 2048, but this version is infinite — keep going as high as you can!
🤖
Watch the AI
Hit the AI button to watch an Expectimax AI play. Use the speed slider to control how fast it goes.
💡 Tip: Keep your highest tile in a corner and build a "snake" of descending values toward it.
